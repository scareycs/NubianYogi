About

The Nubian Yogi has been practicing yoga since 2006. After experiencing some emotional issues, from losing a home, 2 cars, and most of his family possessions as a result of Hurricane Katrina, his wife suggested that he try some yoga classes at their local YMCA. After about the 3rd class, an elderly gentleman (the only other male in the class) communicated that he had buried all his friends and, the only thing that he did different from them was attend yoga. Ever since that moment, The Nubian Yogi has been a consistent practitioner.

In 2015, his journey brought him to Embrace Studio in Washington DC, where he decided to take the 200-hour teacher training under tutelage of the incomparable Faith Hunter. He originally enrolled so that he could deepen his own practice however, shortly after receiving his certificate, Faith asked him to teach a weekly class. To this date, he still teaches that weekly class at Embrace.

The Nubian Yogi is enrolled currently in an additional 300-hour YTT certification class, once again, at Embrace Studio.

He has led classes in such venues as the John F. Kennedy Center, the United States State Department and others. He teaches groups of any size, as well as private classes. Vinyasa, as well as Yen yoga classes are his specialties.
